Glombrox is a member of Globox's species who appears as a playable character in Rayman Origins, Rayman Legends, Rayman Fiesta Run and Rayman Mini. Much like Dark Globox from Rayman Arena, Glombrox seems to be Globox's evil doppelgänger.

Description

Glombrox appears to be an evil clone of Globox, much like how Raymesis is Rayman's evil double. This is supported by the fact that both Raymesis and Glombrox share the colour scheme of purple bodies and entirely yellow eyes, as well as the fact that Glombrox shares his height, fighting style and body shape with Globox. However, Rayman Legends reveals that he is just another member of Globox's species, and described by the Bubble Dreamer as a dangerous criminal with a poor personal hygiene.

Role in the games

Rayman Origins

OK...?! Who let Glombrox out of his cage!? That face...! That smell...! OY! Soap and water do not just bubbles make... Oy, Glombrox, you look positively horrifying.. Darktoons are such a bad influence... Play nice now, or we will smack you silly!
—Bubble Dreamer, Rayman Origins

He is one of the three playable members of Globox's species in the game, and the only one who is not referred as Globox with a different colour. He is the last unlockable character of the game, requiring all 246 Electoon cages to be broken. He was confirmed as a playable character alongside Raymesis and The First King in the Halloween trailer for Rayman Origins.

Rayman Legends

This once great hero has been possessed by Darktoons, and now spends his time trying to scare everyone. But it doesn't work so well as many people mistake him with Globox...
—In-game description, Rayman Legends

He returns as a playable character in Rayman Legends. He can be unlocked once 40000 Lums have been collected in the PC, Xbox 360, PlayStation 3 and PlayStation Vita versions, 55000 Lums in the PlayStation 4 version, 75000 Lums in the Wii U and Nintendo Switch versions, and 100000 Lums in the Xbox One version. The description the game provides for the character reveals that he was a once great hero who ended up being possessed by Darktoons, and now tries to scare inhabitants of the Glade of Dreams, but fails due to his resemblance to Globox.

Rayman Fiesta Run

He returns in Rayman Fiesta Run where he can be purchased for 1500 Lums.

Rayman Adventures

Although he doesn't appear in Rayman Adventures, his files have been found in the game.

Glombrox as he would have appeared in Rayman Adventures.


Rayman Mini

In Rayman Mini he is unlocked for 3 electoons.
