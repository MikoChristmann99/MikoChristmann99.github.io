Description

Rayomz looks identical to Rayman, but he is always naked. He dons a fig leaf over his crotch, much like Adam and Eve in the Biblical Book of Genesis.

Role in the games

Rayman Legends

This guy could give Rayman a bad name! And everybody mistakes him for our hero. But hey! Rayman would never abandon his legendary purple T-shirt!
—In-game description, Rayman Legends, Rayman Adventures

In Rayman Legends he becomes playable once 1 000 000 Lums have been collected. Unlocking him also unlocks the Sooo rich! achievement/trophy.

Rayman Adventures

In Rayman Adventures he can be bought for 1 500 gems.

Rayomz's sharing photo in Rayman Adventures.


Rayman Mini

In Rayman Mini he is unlocked for 4 Swag Coins.
