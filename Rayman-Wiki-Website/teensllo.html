Teensllo is a playable character who was introduced in the Candy World update for Rayman Fiesta Run.

Description

Teensllo shares a resemblance to Grand Minimus, but the only major difference is that Teensllo is made entirely out of blue gelatin.

Role in the games

Rayman Fiesta Run

In Rayman Fiesta Run he can be purchased for 4 000 Lums.

Trivia

  • His name is a portmanteau of "Teensy" and "Jello".
