The Green Teensy is a playable Teensy character in Rayman Origins and Rayman Fiesta Run.

Description

Green Teensy wears a completely green costume, featuring a dark-green coat with lima-coloured edges and a light-green shirt.

Role in the games

Rayman Origins

Let us give thanks for big noses... Nobody inhales flies like you!!! Weren't you king last time I saw you? Oh, that was the other one? I'm confused, are you confused?! Nevermind. Play! Be free! Spin spells! Tell tall tales! Live!
—Bubble Dreamer, Rayman Origins

In Rayman Origins he appears as a playable character from the start. Unlike all other characters unlocked by default (the other ones being Rayman, Globox and Goth Teensy) he can't be seen in the intro. Instead the Grand Minimus is seen, who is an unlockable character. As a possible joke to this inconsistency, the Bubble Dreamer acts surprised at how the Grand Minimus changed to a Green Teensy at the Snoring Tree. The Bubble Dreamer also exalts the Green Teensy for his ability to inhale flies while snoring, though it is only Goth Teensy who is ever seen doing this, which is during the intro.

Several other Green Teensies can be seen in the level Riding the Storm where they help the Nymphs to focus their magic energy to open the path to the Moody Clouds. Much like the Red Wizards from the level Moseying the Mountain, the players can interact with the Green Teensies by slapping them which will result in them slapping back at Rayman and his friends.

Rayman Fiesta Run

In Rayman Fiesta Run he can be purchased for 500 Lums.

Rayman Legends

Several Green Teensies can be seen up on trees during the introduction of the level Once upon a Time in Rayman Legends. This area is invaded by a gang of evil Livingstones, who attack the Teensies violently, and capture them in cages, transported by small dragons.
